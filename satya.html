Satya

Satya is a great name for those in the West who have Indian roots or are looking for a discreet virtue name.
it refers to a virtue in Indian religions, referring to being truthful in one's thought, speech and action.
Name Satya in the Indian origin, means true, pure, another name for Lord Vishnu, Lord Krishna and the asvattha tree, Truth ; Pramod Ganesh
